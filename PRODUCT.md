# PRODUCT.md — MMC Techniek B.V.

## Product Purpose
MMC Techniek B.V. is a Dutch technical installation and home sustainability company. We install heat pumps, solar panels, air conditioning, battery storage, underfloor heating, lifts, and smart electrical systems. We also do full home renovations upgrading to energy label A++.

## Users

**Primary:** Dutch homeowners aged 25–65, with the largest segment at 35–45.
- Both men and women
- Not children or teenagers
- Living in the Netherlands
- Own their home or are renovating
- Care about energy costs, sustainability, and comfort
- Researching before making large investments in their home
- Value trustworthiness, experience, and clear communication

**Secondary:** Business owners seeking commercial building upgrades.

## Tone
- Professional but approachable
- Warm and trustworthy (like a reliable neighbor who happens to be an expert)
- Confident without being pushy
- Dutch directness: clear, no fluff, every word earns its place
- Proud of craftsmanship and 16 years of experience

## Anti-references
- No corporate coldness
- No generic stock-photo energy company vibes
- No greenwashing or eco-pretension
- No cluttered, overwhelming layouts
- No dark themes (this is a daylight decision for homeowners)

## Register
**brand** — This is a marketing/landing site where design IS the product. The site must build trust and convert visitors into quote requests.

## Strategic Principles
1. Trust first: certifications, testimonials, and experience must be immediately visible
2. Services are the hero: show what we do, not just tell
3. Clear path to contact: every page should make reaching out easy
4. Mobile-first: many users browse on phones during evenings
5. Speed matters: homeowners bounce from slow sites
